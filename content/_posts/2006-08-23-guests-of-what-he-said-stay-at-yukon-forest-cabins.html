Carole and I spent our last pre-class weekend (actually, there's still one more to go, but that's the traditional "pre-class panic weekend") in a cabin just south of town run by Yukon Forest Cabins. A sign that the vacation is off to a fine start is when you find a chocolate-chip bundt cake waiting in the cabin.

Fondue for two at the Wolf's Den capped a day of lazy trail hikes and a dip for the dog in Shadow Lake.

So, while visiting/escaping Whitehorse, give one of the forest cabins a try. Just be sure to avoid the snooty neighbours.
