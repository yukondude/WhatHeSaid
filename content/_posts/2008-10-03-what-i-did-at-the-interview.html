I had my first interview in many a year this morning. I have no earthly idea how it went though. It seemed as though I spent most of it floating above myself watching a manic interviewee ramble disjointedly through various hypothetical situations.

But if nothing else, I did manage to answer one conflict-of-interest question by means of the Vulcan Salute, while declaring an eternal truth: "The good of the many outweighs the good of the few, or the one."

So that made it a pretty good interview.
